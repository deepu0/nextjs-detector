# Privacy Policy for Next.js Detector

**Last Updated:** December 2, 2025

The **Next.js Detector** Chrome Extension is designed to respect your privacy.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any user data.**

-   **No Analytics**: We do not track how you use the extension.
-   **No Personal Information**: We do not access or store your IP address, browsing history, or any personal details.
-   **No Remote Servers**: All detection logic runs locally within your browser. The extension does not communicate with any external servers.

## Permissions

The extension requires specific permissions solely for its core functionality:

-   **activeTab**: To detect Next.js on the page you are currently viewing.
-   **scripting**: To check for Next.js global variables in the page context.
-   **storage**: To temporarily store the detected version number locally on your device so it can be displayed in the popup. This data is not synced or sent anywhere.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support tab.
